Privacy Policy

Below you find the Privacy and Cookies Policy for www.thehaukai.co.nz.

We collect personal information from you via our booking and contact forms, including your:
name
contact information
interactions with us.

We collect personal information in order to:
keep in contact with our customers and inform them about offers and recent news related to the The Haukai and gather analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve user experience. Providing some information is optional.

We do not share any information with third-parties about individuals.

If you choose not to enter the ability to store cookies in your web browser we will be unable to use analytics to improve the overall visitor experience. You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@thehaukai.co.nz.
